About Us

We are a team of technology researchers formed in October 2017. We conceptualize, build and deploy new technologies in collaboration with organizations and publish research papers on the results at top conferences around the world, including ACM CSCW, ACM CHI, ACL LREC, and many more.

Enabling the next billion users to become technologically adept

To deploy end-to-end technologies that help the lives of the poorest people around the world

Meet The Team

Devansh Mehta

CEO

Devansh Mehta has broad experience in the social sector, including as an investigative journalist and as a specialist for deploying new technologies at many grassroots NGOs, results of which have been published at top-tier conferences including ACM CSCW and ACM CHI. Devansh recently completed a Master’s Degree in International Affairs from the School of International and Public Affairs (SIPA), Columbia University

Vishnu Prasad

COO

Vishnu Prasad has been working on a peace process in Chhattisgarh that has brought together over 500 people and resulted in dialogue between Maoist insurgents and the government. He has also published a story on the role of traditional health systems in rural and remote areas during the COVID-19 crisis. Vishnu recently completed his Masters in Communication from the University of Hyderabad.

Alok Sharma

Tech Consultant

Alok Sharma is a software engineer who has recently set-up his own software consulting company in Delhi. Alok has worked at the Indian Statistical Institute, the University of Wisconsin, Madison, and is now working on multiple projects with CGnet Swara. Alok received a Bsc in Computer Science from the University of Delhi and a M.Sc. in Mathematics from IIT Kharagpur.
